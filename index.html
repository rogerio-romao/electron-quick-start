THE SPIRAL

Music Player / Visualizer v1.0.0

HELP SCREEN

Not indicated for people susceptible to rapid flashing lights.

Best in full-screen (press F key) and accompanied by your favorite music playlist. If key commands aren't working click on screen first to focus it.

PLAYER: Click Add Track(s) to select one or multiple audio files from your device (as long as the device supports it). The files get added to the playlist (scroll to see as only one gets shown to save space). Use the controls to play, stop etc. and click on the progress bar to skip. Use the P key to hide / show the player. When a track is finished the next one will play and the playlist will loop.

VISUALIZER: Spiral will do it's own thing or you can click, touch or press space bar to create a new spiral. Changes algorithm every 100 seconds by default, press I key to increase by 10 seconds or D to decrease. Or press M to toggle between auto-changing and manual mode. These will take effect after the next change.

The algorithm name is displayed on screen when it changes. Press S for silence mode to toggle it on/off.

H to open / close the help screen.
